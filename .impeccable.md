# .impeccable.md — Cabinet Maître Olivier Chauvel

## Design Context

### Users
Two distinct visitor types arrive on this site:

**Individuals in crisis** — people facing divorce proceedings, criminal charges, personal injury claims, or immigration issues. They arrive anxious, often under emotional strain, searching for a trustworthy professional quickly. They need to feel safe and competent hands before they pick up the phone.

**Professional referrers** — notaries, insurers, doctors, and other professionals who refer clients. They're vetting credibility and expertise. They want clear signals of seriousness and specialisation.

Both need immediate reassurance. The primary emotional goal is **"I'm in good hands"** — not impressed, not excited, not charmed. Safe.

### Brand Personality
Three words: **rigorous · human · grounded**

- Rigorous: 20+ years at the Rennes Bar, five distinct practice areas, transparent fees — competence is documented
- Human: available, listens, explains clearly — not a distant institution
- Grounded: anchored in Rennes, near the Parlement de Bretagne and Cité judiciaire — local, rooted, accessible

The tone is that of a trusted professional in a small city — not BigLaw, not a franchise. More like a well-established GP practice than a corporate firm.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Editorial luxury, warm authority** — the existing direction is correct and should be refined, not reinvented.

**Current palette** (retain and refine):
- Deep navy `#1B2D47` — primary, authoritative, trust signal
- Gold `#9A7332` — accent, signals quality and heritage
- Cream `#F9F7F2` → `#E8DCC4` — warm canvas, never cold white
- Ink `#0F1E32` — text, warm black, never pure #000

**Theme:** Light. Warm cream backgrounds. Visitors arrive in all contexts, often during the day from a phone or desktop, often emotionally stressed. Dark mode would feel cold and clinical.

**Current fonts** (Cormorant Garamond display + DM Sans body):
- Both are in the impeccable banned-font list and should be replaced when fonts are next touched
- Direction to maintain: a refined high-contrast serif for display + a clean, warm sans for body
- Look toward: Spectral, Gilda Display, Libre Baskerville, Bely, Canela (display); Satoshi, Geist, Figtree, Nunito Sans (body) — anything NOT on the reflex list

**Anti-references:**
- Generic law firm template (stock handshake photo, blue/grey, 3-column icon grid)
- Aggressive personal injury advertising (red/black, call-now, ambulance-chaser energy)
- Trendy startup aesthetic (rounded sans, gradient buttons, SaaS product feel)

**References feel:** A well-kept Breton notary office. A regional newspaper's legal section. A good French brasserie menu — dignified without being intimidating.

### Design Principles

1. **Calm authority over performance.** Every design decision should reduce anxiety, not impress. Large quiet spaces, restrained colour use, clear hierarchy.

2. **Warmth through materiality.** Cream surfaces, warm ink, gold used sparingly as a trust signal — never decorative. The site should feel like quality paper, not a screen.

3. **Clarity over cleverness.** Navigation is direct, CTAs say what they do ("Prendre rendez-vous"), content avoids jargon. Visitors in crisis do not puzzle over layouts.

4. **Local and grounded.** Rennes photography, real address, real phone number front and centre. Avoid stock imagery. The specificity of place is a trust signal.

5. **Restraint with accents.** Gold appears at 10% visual weight. Any deviation — extra golds, extra animations, extra decoration — dilutes the signal. Less is always more here.

## Technical Constraints

- Stack: Vanilla React JSX + plain CSS (no build step beyond Vercel static serve)
- No Tailwind — all styles in `styles.css` via CSS custom properties
- Animations: CSS-only via `.reveal` / `fadeUp` keyframes + intersection observer in `app.jsx`
- Images: unoptimized JPGs at root (performance debt — conversion to WebP pending)
- Vercel deployment with SPA rewrite + security headers + asset caching
